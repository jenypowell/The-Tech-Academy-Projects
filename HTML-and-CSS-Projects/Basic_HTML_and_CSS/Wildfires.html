Oregon Wildfires

The wildfires have burned millions of acres in Oregon in 2020.
We had to evacuate for 7 days but luckily we were able to come home!.
There was ash falling all around us while we loaded up our pigs and ducks to bring them to a shelter.
We used a volunteer group called Cowgirl 911© Who were amazing and I cannot thank them enough!

This image is from September 9th at 8:45  AM during level 3 evacuation.
